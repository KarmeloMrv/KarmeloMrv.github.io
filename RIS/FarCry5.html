Characters

The Deputy

The silent protagonist, known simply as "The Deputy," arrives in Hope County to arrest Joseph Seed, becoming embroiled in the conflict with the Eden's Gate cult.

Joseph Seed

The charismatic and ruthless leader of the Eden's Gate cult, who believes he is fulfilling a divine mission to save souls, by any means necessary.

John Seed

One of Joseph Seed's brothers and the head of security for the cult. He controls the eastern region of Hope County with violence and coercion.

Faith Seed

Known as "The Siren" or "The Voice," Faith is Joseph Seed's adoptive sister and manipulates followers using a potent drug known as Bliss, ensuring their unwavering loyalty.

Nick Rye

A crop duster pilot and one of the resistance fighters against Eden's Gate. He joins the Deputy's cause, providing aerial support and fighting for his family's safety.
