Characters

Jason Brody

The protagonist, a young man who must adapt to the island's harsh realities to save his friends.

Vaas Montenegro

The main antagonist, a sadistic pirate leader who embodies chaos and madness.

Citra

The enigmatic leader of the Rakyat tribe, who believes Jason is the key to liberating the island.

Dennis

A Rakyat warrior who becomes Jason's mentor and guide on the island.

Hoyt Volker

The main antagonist of the second half of the game, a ruthless drug lord and slave trader.

Weapons

Flamethrower

A powerful weapon that can set enemies and the environment ablaze.

Compound Bow

A stealthy and versatile weapon that can take down enemies silently.

Light Machine Gun

A high-capacity weapon ideal for suppressing fire and taking down multiple enemies.

AK-47

A reliable assault rifle favored by many mercenaries. It is known for its durability and firepower.

Flame Thrower

A weapon that can set enemies and the environment on fire, causing widespread damage.

Desert Eagle

A powerful pistol known for its high stopping power and precision.
