Navigate a War-Torn African Landscape

Far Cry 2 immerses you in the heart of a fictional African country engulfed in civil war, where you play as a mercenary on a mission to eliminate a notorious arms dealer.

Open World Chaos

Explore vast landscapes filled with dynamic weather, wildlife, and a reactive world that responds to your actions.

Moral Ambiguity

Navigate through morally gray decisions and alliances as you confront the brutal realities of war and the consequences of your choices.

Survival and Strategy

Utilize a range of weapons, vehicles, and tactics to survive in a hostile environment where every decision could mean life or death.

Hunt Down the Jackal

Track and eliminate the enigmatic arms dealer known as the Jackal, whose influence and operations fuel the conflict tearing the country apart.
