Survive the Unknown

Far Cry takes you to a remote tropical archipelago where you, Jack Carver, find yourself hunted by mercenaries and mutated creatures in a fight for survival.

Navigate through lush forests, ancient ruins, and hidden caves as you uncover the dark secrets of the mysterious islands.

Face off against well-equipped mercenaries and mutated creatures, each posing unique challenges and threats to your survival.

Escape or Die Trying

Survive deadly encounters, utilize stealth and strategy, and uncover an escape plan amidst the chaos and danger.

Characters

Jack Carver

The protagonist, a former special forces operative turned boat skipper. Jack finds himself fighting for survival on a remote island.

Val Cortez

A CIA agent undercover as a journalist. Val teams up with Jack to uncover the secrets of the island and the mercenaries.

Dr. Krieger

The main antagonist, a mad scientist conducting genetic experiments on the island. Dr. Krieger's actions have dire consequences for everyone involved.

Doyle

A mysterious contact who provides Jack with information and assistance throughout his journey.

Colonel Crowe

Dr. Krieger's right-hand man, a mercenary leader who oversees the security operations on the island. He is a relentless adversary to Jack.

Weapons

Machete

A versatile melee weapon used for close combat and clearing foliage.

M4

A standard-issue rifle used by mercenaries. It offers a good balance of firepower and accuracy.

Sniper Rifle

A long-range weapon perfect for taking out enemies from a distance with precision.

Jackhammer

A powerful close-quarters weapon, ideal for taking down enemies at short range.

Rocket Launcher

A high-explosive weapon for dealing with groups of enemies.

Desert Eagle

A reliable sidearm for quick and precise encounters.
