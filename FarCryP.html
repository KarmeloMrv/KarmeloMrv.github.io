FAR CRY PRIMAL

Welcome to the Far Cry Primal Showcase. Journey to the Stone Age and survive in a world ruled by primitive tribes and deadly predators.

Explore Oros

Far Cry Primal takes you back to Oros, a lush but dangerous world filled with rival tribes, deadly wildlife, and ancient mysteries.

Survive and Thrive

Adapt to the harsh environment as you hunt for food, craft weapons, and build your tribe to secure a future in Oros.

Confront Rival Tribes

Battle against rival tribes like the Udam and Izila who threaten your existence and dominance in the prehistoric world.

Tame Prehistoric Beasts

Domesticate and command wild beasts such as sabertooth tigers and mammoths to aid you in combat and exploration.

Uncover Ancient Mysteries

Discover the secrets of Oros and its mystical artifacts, unlocking the power to control fire and other supernatural abilities.

Characters

Takkar

The protagonist, a skilled hunter of the Wenja tribe, on a quest to unite his people and protect them from rival tribes.

Ull

The fearsome leader of the Udam tribe, driven by a primal thirst for conquest and survival at any cost.

Batatata

The cunning and ambitious leader of the Izila tribe, wielding the power of fire and striving to dominate Oros.

Sayla

A shaman of the Wenja tribe, gifted with spiritual insight and guiding Takkar on his journey through the primal world.

Badari

A skilled warrior and loyal companion of Takkar, fighting alongside him to protect the Wenja tribe.

Weapons

Spear

A versatile melee weapon crafted from stone and bone, essential for hunting and combat in Oros.

Bow

A ranged weapon used for hunting and warfare, capable of firing arrows with deadly precision.

Clubs

Primitive clubs made from hardened wood and bone, ideal for close-quarters combat against enemies and wildlife.

Bombs

Improvised explosives crafted from natural materials, used for tactical advantage and surprising enemies.

Fire Bow

A specialized bow equipped with fire arrows, capable of igniting enemies and setting fire to structures.

Tamed Sabertooth Tiger

A fearsome predator tamed by Takkar to assist in battles and intimidate enemies in the wilds of Oros.
