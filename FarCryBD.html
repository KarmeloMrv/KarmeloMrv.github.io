FAR CRY 3: BLOOD DRAGON

Welcome to the Far Cry 3: Blood Dragon Showcase. Dive into the retro-futuristic story, characters, and weapons of this unique game.

Enter the Retro-Futuristic Cyber Nightmare

Far Cry Blood Dragon delivers a unique 80s-inspired spin on the Far Cry series, blending humor, neon aesthetics, and over-the-top action in a dystopian future.

Neon Drenched World

Explore a vibrant and surreal open world filled with neon-lit landscapes, futuristic technology, and mutated creatures.

Cybernetic Powers

Unleash cybernetic enhancements and advanced weaponry to take down rogue military forces and monstrous creations threatening humanity.

Retro Humor and Parody

Experience a humorous narrative filled with references to 80s pop culture, cheesy one-liners, and exaggerated action sequences.

Save the World

Take on the role of Sergeant Rex Power Colt, a cybernetic commando, and thwart a mad scientist's plan to unleash global chaos with a devastating weapon.

Characters

Rex Colt

The protagonist, a cyber-commando tasked with stopping the rogue army. Rex is a parody of action heroes from the 80s.

Dr. Elizabeth Darling

A scientist who assists Rex in his mission. She provides intelligence and support throughout the game.

Colonel Sloan

The main antagonist, a former commander of Rex who has gone rogue and leads the army of cyborgs.

Weapons

AJM 9

A high-powered pistol based on the classic Desert Eagle, known for its futuristic look and stopping power.

Galleria 1991

A powerful shotgun that is highly effective at close range.

Kobracon

A sniper rifle that shoots plasma bolts, perfect for long-range engagements.

Minigun

A rapid-fire weapon that can mow down enemies with ease.

Phazertron

A futuristic assault rifle with high accuracy and rate of fire.

Flamethrower

A weapon that can set enemies and the environment on fire, causing widespread damage.
