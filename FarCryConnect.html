Far Cry Connect

Enhance your Far Cry experience with the companion app. Manage missions, track progress, connect with friends, and access exclusive rewards.

Features

  • Interactive Maps: Explore detailed maps and set waypoints.
  • Mission Tracking: Manage and track your missions.
  • Game Stats: View your game statistics and performance metrics.
  • Rewards and Challenges: Access exclusive rewards and daily challenges.
  • Social Features: Connect with friends and the Far Cry community.
  • Custom Loadouts: Customize your character's loadout and gear.
  • Game Guides: Access tutorials and tips from expert players.
  • Push Notifications: Stay updated with the latest game news and events.
