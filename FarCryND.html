FAR CRY NEW DAWN

Welcome to the Far Cry New Dawn Showcase. Explore a vibrant post-apocalyptic world filled with danger, beauty, and new beginnings.

Explore Hope County

Far Cry New Dawn returns to the post-apocalyptic setting of Hope County, Montana, years after the nuclear catastrophe known as "the Collapse."

Survival and Rebuilding

Embark on a journey of survival and resilience as you rebuild settlements and face off against ruthless scavengers and the Highwaymen gang.

Confront the Twins

Battle against the twin sisters, Mickey and Lou, leaders of the Highwaymen, who seek to control the dwindling resources of Hope County.

Explore the New Frontier

Discover a transformed landscape where nature has reclaimed the ruins of civilization, presenting both beauty and new threats.

Form Alliances

Forge alliances with surviving communities and charismatic leaders to strengthen your position against the chaos of the post-apocalyptic world.

Characters

Carmina Rye

The daughter of Nick Rye and Kim Rye, key to the rebuilding efforts of Prosperity and the fight against the Highwaymen.

Mickey and Lou

The ruthless twin sisters leading the Highwaymen gang, determined to dominate the new world with violence and exploitation.

Bean

A quirky survivalist and historian, obsessed with collecting artifacts and preserving the knowledge of the old world.

Thomas Rush

A charismatic engineer and leader of the survivors, driving the effort to rebuild and defend against the Highwaymen's threats.

Nana

An elderly sharpshooter and former sniper, providing invaluable combat skills and wisdom to the resistance against the Highwaymen.

Weapons

Saw Launcher

A devastating weapon that fires circular saw blades, capable of bouncing off surfaces and hitting multiple targets.

Compound Bow

A versatile bow equipped with high-tech enhancements, allowing for silent takedowns and precise long-range shots.

AK-47

A classic assault rifle modified and upgraded for the new world, reliable in combat against both human and mutant enemies.

Dynamite

Explosive devices used for clearing obstacles and dealing heavy damage to fortified positions and vehicles.

Explosive Arrow

An arrow equipped with an explosive tip, perfect for taking out groups of enemies or destroying enemy vehicles.

Sawmill

A heavy machine gun mounted on a vehicle or turret, capable of sustained fire to suppress enemies and protect allies.
