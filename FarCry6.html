Far Cry 6 takes you to Yara, a tropical paradise frozen in time, ruled by a ruthless dictator, Anton Castillo.

Characters

Dani Rojas

The protagonist, a local Yaran caught up in the revolution against Anton Castillo's regime.

Anton Castillo

The ruthless dictator of Yara, determined to restore the nation to its former glory, no matter the cost.

Diego Castillo

Anton Castillo's son, groomed to succeed his father and carry on the legacy of the regime.

Clara Garcia

The leader of the Libertad, the guerrilla movement fighting against Anton Castillo's regime.

Juan Cortez

An ex-CIA agent turned guerrilla mentor, guiding Dani Rojas in the fight against the regime.

Weapons

RPG-7

SPAS-15

A semi-automatic shotgun renowned for its power and efficiency in close-range combat.

Tank

A heavily armored military vehicle equipped with powerful cannons and machine guns, capable of dominating the battlefield.

Helicopter

Use helicopters for aerial maneuvers and swift transport across Yara's vast terrain.
